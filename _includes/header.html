Chris Ilott
Visual Merchandiser and Stylist.

Chris commenced his VM career at UNIQLO's premiere Australian store, Emporium Melbourne, having completed RMIT's industry recognised Diploma of Visual Merchandising. Whilst studying, Chris gained extensive practical experience interning at Australian fashion icons Country Road, Seed Heritage and Sportsgirl, and volunteering as an event stylist with Georgeous Occasions. His diploma is complemented by a prior Bachelor of Business and more than five years retail experience.

Chris is currently a Visual Merchandise Supervisor with UNIQLO Australia.
